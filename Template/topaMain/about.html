Nikita Deuja

Founder & Creative Director

Nikita Deuja is a Fashion Designer originally from Kathmandu, Nepal. She is an alumni of Fashion Institute of Technology, New York with AAS and BFA degree in fashion design and specialization in knitwear. She is a sustainable designer who loves working with unconventional materials.

She has knowledge of hand and machine knitting, draping, sewing, patternmaking, Photoshop, Illustrator, Design Research, Mood board creating, Market Research, PLM, Tech Pack, CLO 3D, STOLL M1, Kaledo print, weave & knit, Digital CAD.
